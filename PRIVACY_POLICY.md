# **Privacy Policy for Smart Calculator App**

**Last Updated:** **22 November 2025**


## **1. Introduction**

Thank you for using **Smart Calculator**.  
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information in a clear and transparent manner.



## **2. Information We Collect**

We **do not collect, store, process, or share** any personal or sensitive user data.

The app does **NOT**:

- Collect personal information  
- Track user behavior or analytics  
- Request unnecessary permissions  
- Share data with third parties  

All core features such as **basic calculations**, **scientific functions**, and **unit conversions** work completely **offline**.



## **3. Local Processing Only**

All operations and calculations are performed **locally on your device**.  
No data is transmitted over the internet or sent to external servers.



## **4. Permissions**

The app may request minimal permissions required for specific functionality (e.g., vibration control).  
However, **none of these permissions collect or share any user data**.



## **5. Third-Party Services**

This app does **not** integrate:

- Third-party SDKs  
- Analytics or tracking tools  
- Advertising libraries  
- Online data services  

No information is ever shared with any third-party providers.



## **6. Children’s Privacy**

The application does **not** collect any data from users, including children under the age of 13.  
It is safe and appropriate for users of all ages.



## **7. Data Security**

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no risk of your data being accessed, leaked, or misused.



## **8. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.  
Any changes will be reflected by updating the **“Last Updated”** date at the top of this document.
